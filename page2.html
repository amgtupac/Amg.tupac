Mission And Vision

Mission

 RMU is to provide high-quality education and training in maritime fields, develop competent professionals, promote research and innovation, foster regional cooperation, and contribute to the socio-economic development of its member states through skilled manpower in the maritime industry.

Vision

The vision of the Regional Maritime University is to become a leading center of excellence for maritime and allied fields education and training in the West and Central African sub-region and beyond. It aims to promote sustainable development through advanced maritime knowledge and technical expertise.
